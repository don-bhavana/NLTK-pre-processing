NLTK Pre-Processing



Step-1

Tokenizer

Tokenization is the process of turning sensitive data into nonsensitive data called "tokens" that can be used in a database or internal system without bringing it into scope.



Step-2

Stop Word Removal

Stop words are commonly used words like 'the', 'a', 'an', 'in', etc. Since stop words occur in abundance and providing little to no unique information that can be used for classification or clustering,we remove them.Firstly we elimate all the alpha numeric,special characters. Then,we convert all the letters into lower case.Finally,we remove all the stop words by comparing our input text with the stop word corpus.
